J - rotate blue sphere
7 - turn on/off shadows
8 - fill the screen with red color
M - add posteffect "Monochrome"
N - add posteffect "Saturate"
K - enable/disable DOF
С - clear all posteffects
L - enable/disable procedural textures
O - enable/disable normal mapping
P - turn on Strauss lightning model
B - turn on Cook-Torrance lightning model
V - turn on standard lightning model
Z - look from light source

Перемещение:
W - forward
S - backward
A - strafe left
D - strafe right
Q - strafe up
E - strafe down
Mousedown + mouse movement == rotation of the camera.
